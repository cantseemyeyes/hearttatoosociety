Businesses

Resutaurants can partner with us during food drives. Don't throw out your food! Send them our way! Corporations can also join us for volunteer events to fulfill Corporate Social Responsibility. We'll sign off your documents once your volunteer experience is complete.

Charities

Charities with similar goals can partner with The Heart Tattoo Society to achieve common goals. If you have an idea to collaborate, send us a message. We're always open to new ideas!

Student Organizations

Students organizations can find meaningful ways to collaborate with The Heart Tattoo Society. If it make sense, we'll find a way to make it work. Our website for example was built by a student for his school project!

Individuals

Don't feel discouraged if you are not affiliated with any organizations. We are always looking for talented individuals with a passion for helping displaced people in the Vancouver area. Take a look at our website. It was built by a student for his school project!

Send us a donation.

If you are unable to volunteer, you can still donate directly to our charity. Please don't hesitate to send us a message for inquiries!
