Help Your Local Community

The Heart Tattoo Society primarily supports marginalized individuals in the Downtown Eastside through community meal programs. This is not to say that we don't accept new ideas. If you had something different in mind, feel free to email us directly. We are always looking for creative ways to improve the lives of displaced people in the Vancouver Downtown Eastside.
