The Heart Tattoo Society

Our Community. Your Impact. Making a positive impact on the lives of displaced people in the Vancouver downtown area.

Donate Now! Get Involved!

Our Story

The Heart Tattoo Society primarily supports displaced people in the Downtown Eastside through donations, events, partnerships, and word of mouth. This is not to say that we don't accept new ideas. If you had something different in mind, feel free to email us directly with your ideas. We are always looking for creative ways to improve the lives of displaced people in the Vancouver Downtown Eastside.

Our Community

The Heart Tattoo Society wants to reach out to anyone that needs a helping hand. However, this can not be done without the community. Whether it be dropping off food/clothing/medical donations, fundraising, or scouring craigslist for free items a family could use. The Heart Tattoo Society wants everyone that helps to be recognized through our facebook page as this is a community effort.
